Title of Project: Read and Rate Sentences

Request to Participate in Research

We would like to invite you to participate in a web-based online survey. The survey is part of a research study whose purpose is to understand how English speakers rate sentences. This survey should take 20–25 minutes to complete. We are asking you to participate in this study because you are a native English speaker located in the United States. You must be at least 18 years old to take this survey.

The decision to participate in this research project is voluntary. You do not have to participate and you can refuse to answer any question. Even if you begin the web-based online survey, you can stop at any time. Payment will not be made if you fail to complete the HIT or if you fail to provide attentive answers to survey questions.

There are no foreseeable risks or discomforts to you for taking part in this study.

You will be paid $4.00 for completion of this HIT through the Amazon Mechanical Turk platform.

Your part in this study is anonymous to the researchers. However, because of the nature of web based surveys, it is possible that respondents could be identified by the IP address or other electronic record associated with the response. Neither the researcher nor anyone involved with this survey will be capturing those data. Any reports or publications based on this research will use only group data and will not identify you or any individual as being affiliated with this project. Your data are also stored on Mechanical Turk's servers, and the data there are subject to the Amazon Mechanical Turk Privacy Notice and Participation Agreement.
